Safety Tips

Check with someone reliable first.

Before going out, make sure that where you're going is safe. While everyone has a different sense of what safe is, it's helpful to have an opinion or two before you go.

Plan for getting there and getting home.

Plan ahead. Arrange your transport to and from your destination and tell a friend or family member your plans.

Safewords at Bars

Some bars have safewords you can tell the bartender when you are feeling unsafe.
